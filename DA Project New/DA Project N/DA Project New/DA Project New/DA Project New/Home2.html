Statistics of the Most Played sport

In recent years, of the relatively small group of people in the United States aged 15 years and older who engaged in sports or exercise activities on an average day, about 30 percent walked for exercise, making walking the most popular form of exercise overall. Basketball was the team sport that people engaged in most frequently. On an average day, 5 percent of those who exercised played basketball.
